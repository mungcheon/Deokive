# Deokive Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-20

Deokive ("the App") is a fandom archive application for managing goods, folders, profile settings, and optional Google account integration.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App may collect or store the following information depending on the features you use:

- Profile information you enter, such as nickname, tag, and profile image
- Goods, folders, calendar entries, and support inquiries created in the App
- Google account basic profile information when you choose Google sign-in
- App settings such as language, theme, notification preferences, and sign-in preferences

## 2. How Information Is Stored

- Most app data is stored locally on your device.
- If you sign in with Google and enable Google Drive backup features, Deokive may store backup data in your Google Drive app data storage area.
- Google Drive backups are used only to store your Deokive app data for restore and continuity purposes.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the stored information to:

- Provide account sign-in and profile features
- Save and restore your Deokive data
- Sync backup data to your Google Drive when Google backup is enabled
- Improve stability, usability, and support response quality

## 4. Google Data Access

If you use Google sign-in, the App may access:

- Your basic Google account profile information
- Your Google Drive app data folder, only for Deokive backup files

The App does not use Google Drive access to read unrelated user files.

## 5. Sharing of Information

Deokive does not sell your personal information.

Information is not shared with third parties except:

- When required to provide Google sign-in or Google Drive backup functionality
- When required by law, regulation, or valid legal request

## 6. Data Retention

- Local data remains on your device until you delete it, remove the app, or overwrite it.
- Google Drive backup data remains in your Google account until removed or replaced by you or the App.

## 7. Your Choices

You may:

- Use the App without Google sign-in
- Remove local data by deleting the app data from your device
- Stop using Google backup by signing out of your Google account in the App

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app operator through the support email or contact method published with the app.
